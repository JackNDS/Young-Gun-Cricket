1-1 Coaching

The fundamental aspect of YoungGun Cricket. Cultivating relationships and player development.

High Performance Package

  • Our High Performance coaching sessions are conducted by Level 3 coaches or Level 2 coaches with first class or higher playing experience.

  • Coaches will at times utilise some of our technology to help analyze and review player performances.

  • All video footage is kept on file.

  • Cost: $1000 (weekly booking for a 10 week term)

Foundation Package

  • Sessions are conducted by a Level 1 or 2 coach.
  • Developed for young cricketers just starting out in the game (ages 9-12).

  • Focuses are foundations of the game, efficient movement patterns, structure and set up.
  • Sessions can be a single skill or combination of skills.

  • Cost: $800 (weekly booking for a 10 week term)

One Off Coaching Sessions

  • While we believe that consistency and repetition are two of the keys to long term development, we also understand the busy nature of life. As such we do offer one off coaching sessions for those of you looking to work on particular aspects of your game.

  • To book one of these sessions, please send us a message.

  • Cost: Pricing: $120 (45 Minute Session)

Elite Coaching Session

  • Elite coaching sessions are conducted exclusively by a Level 3 Coach.
  • They feature the use of our state of the art technology, including 360 degree, video capture systems.

  • Real time analysis of every aspect of your skillset.
  • Sessions are 45 minutes in duration.

  • Cost: $150
