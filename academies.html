Off Season Foundation Academy

Off Season High Performance Academy

Term 2, 2026:

  • The Under 16 Academy focuses on developing the skills required in representative cricket. It will run on every Friday in Term 2 from 6.30pm - 8.00pm beginning on the 24th April.


Off Season Professional Cricket Pathway

Term 2, 2026:

  • The PCP is designed to provide young aspiring cricketers with an understanding of what high-performance training entails. It will run on Fridays for the duration of Term 2 from 8.00pm - 9.30pm beginning on the 24th of April.
