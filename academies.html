YGC Academies

Holistic coaching programmes developed for players seeking their way to the professional level
All five academies run for the full school term.

Foundation Academy

Term 3, 2025:

  • The Foundation academy is designed for young players looking to explore and learn the basics of the modern game. It will run 5.30pm - 7pm on Friday nights starting on the 25th of July.




Wicket Keeper Academy

Term 3, 2025:

  • TThe Wicket Keeper academy will take place on Friday nights 7pm-8.30pm starting on the 25th of July. Players will alternate each week between a keeping focus and a batting focus.

All-Rounder Academy

Term 3, 2025:

  • The All-Rounders academy will take place on Friday nights starting on the 25th of July. They will alternate weeks being a part of the bowling academy one week and the batting the next week. Players will focus on getting their skills ready for the start of the season while exploring game scenarios for skill development.

Batting Academy

Term 3, 2025:

  • The Batting academy will take place on Friday nights 8pm-9.30pm starting on the 25th of July. Players will focus on getting their skills ready for the start of the season while exploring game scenarios for skill development.

Bowling Academy

Term 3, 2025:

  • The Bowling academy will take place on Friday nights 7pm-8.30pm starting on the 25th of July. Players will focus on getting their skills ready for the start of the season while exploring game scenarios for skill development.
